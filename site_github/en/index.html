AI Video Production

Everything you need to know
in a few questions

AI video production uses generative artificial intelligence to create advertising films, brand content and visual campaigns without a traditional film set. At IKONIK, AI is not a filter applied after the fact — it is the primary creative medium, mastered shot by shot.

An IKONIK AI film is delivered in 3 to 7 days, at a fraction of the cost of an equivalent traditional production. Not because we cut corners — because our AI pipeline eliminates casting, location, set and physical set design costs, without sacrificing cinematic quality.

Full guide — AI Video Production →

FOOH (Fake Out Of Home) uses AI to integrate impossible objects or characters into real urban environments — a giant perfume bottle walking across a bridge, a champagne bottle exploding over a city skyline. These videos generate millions of organic views because they trigger an immediate reaction: "is this real or fake?"

IKONIK produces FOOH by combining real footage of the environment with AI-generated fantastic elements, using the best tools the market has to offer.

Full guide — FOOH →

AI video generates visual sequences through generative artificial intelligence models, without a physical set or actors. Real filming uses cameras, lighting and crews on location.

IKONIK masters both approaches and combines them as needed: real filming for human authenticity and interviews, AI video for impossible scenarios, special effects and cost reduction. The choice depends on the brief, the budget and the creative objective.

An AI video pipeline is an automated production workflow that chains together several specialised artificial intelligence models to create a film from end to end. IKONIK's proprietary pipeline includes: brief analysis, storyboard generation, character sheet creation, video synthesis, shot-by-shot technical review, and final post-production.

This pipeline allows a complete advertising film to be delivered in 3 to 7 days — compared to 4 to 6 weeks for an equivalent traditional production.

AI UGC (User Generated Content by artificial intelligence) refers to authentic creator-style videos generated entirely by AI. These contents imitate customer testimonials, unboxings and spontaneous tutorials that perform naturally on social media.

IKONIK AI UGC costs up to 10 times less than working with real influencers, with the same engagement performance and no contractual constraints.

Full guide — AI UGC →

IKONIK uses a selection of the best artificial intelligence tools available on the market, chosen for each stage of production: reference image generation, cinematic video synthesis, effects and transitions, AI voiceover and sound design, and final post-production. The stack evolves constantly to integrate new models as they are released — that is part of the craft.

The budget for an AI video production depends on many parameters: film duration, sequence complexity, number of characters, formats delivered, integration of real footage. Every project is unique — contact us for a quote tailored to your brief.

Delivery time: 3 to 7 business days for a pure AI production. This breaks down into 1 day of brief and art direction, 1 day of visual reference generation, 2 to 4 days of production and iterations, 1 to 2 days of editing and post-production.
