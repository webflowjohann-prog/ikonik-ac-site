Guide — AI UGC

AI UGC —
User Generated Content by AI

Customer testimonials, unboxings, tutorials — created entirely by artificial intelligence. AI UGC reproduces the authentic, spontaneous style of content creators, without casting, without filming, without contractual constraints.

What is AI UGC?

AI UGC — User Generated Content by artificial intelligence — refers to video content created entirely by AI to mimic the style of independent content creators. These videos reproduce the raw, spontaneous and authentic aesthetic of "homemade" content that performs naturally on social media.

The term "User Generated Content" originally refers to content created by real users — customer testimonials filmed on a smartphone, product unboxings, how-to tutorials, spontaneous reviews. These formats achieve higher engagement rates than traditional advertising because they look like organic content, not brand communications.

AI UGC reproduces exactly that aesthetic — without the constraints that come with real humans: availability, image rights, brand consistency, production volume, influence costs.

Why UGC performs
on social media

The algorithms of social platforms — Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts — reward content that generates authentic engagement. Traditional format advertising is identified as such and scrolled past. UGC-format content is perceived as organic and retains attention.

What brands get with UGC

  • Higher engagement rates — UGC format generates on average 4 times more engagement than traditional display advertising on the same platforms.
  • Lower acquisition cost — Advertising campaigns based on UGC achieve lower CPM and CPC than classic advertising formats.
  • Perceived credibility — A spontaneously filmed customer testimonial is perceived as more credible than an ad spot — even when the viewer knows it is advertising.
  • Versatility — The UGC format adapts to all products, all sectors, all audiences.

AI UGC does not replace human influence in all cases. For brands that need a celebrity or a highly identified creator, human partnership remains relevant. For high-volume content production, A/B message testing, international markets, or brands starting a UGC strategy, AI UGC is the most effective solution.

AI UGC formats
IKONIK produces

Customer testimonials

A synthetic character speaks about the product in the first person, in a realistic domestic or lifestyle setting. Spontaneous tone, natural gestures, authentic wording. 9:16 format, 30 to 60 seconds.

Unboxing and product discovery

Opening packaging, discovering features, first use. The unboxing format performs particularly well on TikTok and Instagram Reels for physical products.

How-to tutorials

Step-by-step product demonstration, usage tips, before/after comparisons. These formats educate the potential customer and reduce purchase friction.

Reviews and comparisons

Product analysis, advantages, key strengths — in the style of an organic creator review. These formats are particularly effective for considered purchase decisions.

"AI UGC is not trying to deceive — it is trying to perform. A viewer who knows it is AI can still be convinced by the message. What matters is content relevance, not content origin."

For which brands
and which uses?

AI UGC is suited to any brand that wants to accelerate its social media content production without multiplying influencer partnerships. Particularly relevant for:

  • E-commerce — Content volume per SKU, message testing, adaptation by market.
  • Beauty and wellness — Tutorials, before/after, usage tips. Formats that educate and convert.
  • Food and beverage — Product discovery, consumption moments, recipes and uses.
  • Tech and apps — Usage demonstrations, feature tutorials, adoption testimonials.
  • Services — Customer satisfaction testimonials, before/after, user journeys.
