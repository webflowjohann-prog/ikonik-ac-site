AI Video Production —
Films made with artificial intelligence

Creating an advertising film without a set, without physical casting, without real locations — and achieving cinema-quality results. That is what AI video production makes possible. Here is what it is, how it works, and what IKONIK does with it.

What is AI video production?

AI video production refers to the creation of video content — advertising films, brand content, animations, social campaigns — using generative artificial intelligence models. These models generate complete visual sequences from text instructions and reference images, without requiring a traditional physical shoot.

It does not replace cinema. It opens a creative territory that cinema cannot reach: impossible sets, perfectly consistent synthetic characters, special effects accessible at any budget scale, and unprecedented execution speed.

AI video production is now used by fashion, luxury, hospitality, real estate and consumer brands to produce advertising films, social media content, viral FOOH campaigns and brand documentaries.

AI video vs traditional production —
what really changes

The fundamental difference is not technical — it is creative and economic. Traditional production is constrained by reality: what can be filmed, where, with whom, in how much time. AI video production is constrained only by imagination.

How does
an AI video pipeline work?

An AI video pipeline is a production workflow that chains several specialised artificial intelligence models together to create a film from end to end. Each step feeds the next — the result is a coherent, controlled, professional-quality film.

IKONIK's 6-step pipeline

  • Strategic brief — Analysis of brand DNA, objectives and constraints. The creative territory is defined before a single image is generated.
  • Art direction — Moodboard, palette, shot-by-shot storyboard. Character sheets for all characters. Every frame is decided before it is generated.
  • AI video generation — Production of sequences using the best tools the market offers. Composition Lock method for visual consistency between shots. Dual reference system: character + composition.
  • Technical review — Shot-by-shot quality control: ratio, fluidity, visual consistency. Best takes selected, iterations where needed.
  • Editing & sound design — Assembly, colour grading, soundtrack, voiceover if required. The film takes its final shape.
  • Multi-format delivery — Export in 16:9, 9:16, 1:1 and 4:5. Masters ready to publish on all platforms.

"We don't generate videos. We produce films. The difference: art direction owned on every single frame, visual consistency across every sequence, and a result that doesn't immediately read as artificial."

What AI video
makes possible

Scenarios impossible on a real shoot

AI video frees production from the constraints of the physical world. A product in an environment that does not yet exist. A character in an inaccessible location. A past era reconstructed with precision. Atmospheric effects that are uncontrollable in reality — mist, raking light, a starlit sky — produced on demand.

FOOH — Fake Out Of Home

FOOH uses artificial intelligence to integrate objects or characters at monumental scale into real urban environments. These videos generate massive organic engagement because they trigger an immediate reaction: "is this real or fake?" — and the answer barely matters. What counts is that the brand is in the conversation.

AI UGC — User Generated Content

Customer testimonials, unboxings and tutorials generated by artificial intelligence, in the authentic and spontaneous style of independent content creators. These formats perform naturally on social media because they look like organic content — not advertising.

Hybrid films — real footage + AI

The most powerful combination for brands that want the authenticity of reality and the creativity of AI. The shoot captures people, emotions, textures. The AI creates the sets, atmospheres and impossible scenes. The final film is indistinguishable.

For which brands
and which objectives?

AI video production is particularly suited to brands that currently produce too little video content due to budget or deadline constraints — and to brands that want to explore creative territories that traditional filming cannot reach.

  • Fashion and luxury — Collection films, image campaigns, animated lookbooks. Dream sets, perfect characters, impossible atmospheres.
  • Food service and gastronomy — Brand films, atmospheres, product showcasing. AI generates worlds that elevate the experience without a costly set.
  • Premium real estate — Photo animation, enriched virtual tours, programme presentation films. Automated pipeline for large-scale production.
  • FMCG and e-commerce — AI UGC, viral FOOH, social media content at volume. Accelerated publishing cadence without exploding costs.
  • Institutional and corporate — Brand documentaries, corporate films, internal communications. AI enriches real footage in post-production.
